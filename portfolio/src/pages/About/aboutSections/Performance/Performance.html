Performance

Alongside with the responsiveness and the animations, performance is the thing that I pay attention to the most during the development

You can see the animations and the responsiveness with the naked eye. And what about the performance? By the performance I mean high FPS value during the attendance of a website, which means that I have to use very specific approaches in styling. In the front-end development, there are a lot of ways to achieve the same goal, but only an experienced developer knows, what they should use to prevent FPS drops and a long rendering time.

Except of the overall smoothness of a website, not less important are such criterios as fast loading speed, accessability and SEO. To improve these measures, I use the PageSpeed Insights, a service by Google. I have mastered the using of it, so I guarantee very high, 80+ points, measurement values.
